Admission

Admission Information

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying examination
  • Valid score in the entrance examination (if applicable)
  • Meeting the age criteria as per the regulations

Application Process

To apply for admission, follow the steps below:

  1. Visit the college website and navigate to the Admission page.
  2. Fill out the online application form with the required details.
  3. Upload the necessary documents, such as educational certificates and photographs.
  4. Pay the application fee online through the provided payment gateway.
  5. Submit the completed application form.

Important Dates

Mark the following important dates for the admission process:

  • Application Start Date: DD/MM/YYYY
  • Application Deadline: DD/MM/YYYY
  • Entrance Examination Date: DD/MM/YYYY
  • Declaration of Results: DD/MM/YYYY
  • Admission Counseling: DD/MM/YYYY
