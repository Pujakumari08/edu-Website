Scholarship Information

Merit-Based Scholarships

We offer merit-based scholarships to recognize and reward academic excellence. Eligibility criteria for merit-based scholarships include:

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 in the previous academic year
  • Outstanding performance in extracurricular activities
  • Strong recommendation letters

Need-Based Scholarships

We provide need-based scholarships to support students with financial constraints. Eligibility criteria for need-based scholarships include:

  • Demonstrated financial need based on income and family circumstances
  • Submission of financial documents, including income statements and tax returns
  • Statement of financial need and personal essay

External Scholarships

In addition to our internal scholarships, students may also explore external scholarship opportunities. These scholarships are offered by organizations, foundations, and government bodies. We recommend visiting the following websites for more information:

  • Scholarships.com
  • Fastweb
  • Cappex
